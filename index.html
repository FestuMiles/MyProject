    Welcome to Genius

    A place where Science, Education, and Leadership converge to empower individuals with cutting-edge STEM skills! To bring the future into the now by harnessing technology-based solutions is part of our goals. Join this exciting journey toward innovation, creativity, and community. Smile at the future with Genius!

    What We Do

    At Genius Education we provide hands-on learning experiences in key emerging technologies like computer science, artificial intelligence, web3, cybersecurity, and more. Our approach focuses on interactive and practical learning methods and This is achieved at our Genius Academy programs such as science fairs, STEM camps, code camps, curriculum development and educators upskilling, and innovation hubs. We also build the capacity of educators and institutions to deliver quality STEM education. Through professional development programs, training workshops, and mentorship initiatives, we aim to empower teachers with the necessary skills and resources to effectively engage students in STEM learning. Additionally, we carry out STEM and educational research through our Research fellowships.

    Project 4IR

    4th Industrial Revolution

    Under our Project 4th Industrial Revolution 4IR, we are working to empower up to 6 million Zambian young people with skills that are directly relevant to the future of work such as artificial intelligence, web3, cybersecurity, big data, design thinking, quantum sciences and 3d as well as nanotechnologies, to name these. This is achieved at our Genius Academy programming. Additionally, we are working on a 4th Industrial Revolution curriculum that exists in hardcopy textbooks as well as web and application based E-learning. Our curriculum harnesses collective intelligence and interconnects loops between Creativity, Innovation, Design, Engineering, Technology, Industry, Leadership and Ethics. The curriculum explores concepts of Neuroscience and Psychology as a tool to unlock the inherent genius of Zambian youth.

    Goals

    1.

    To promote and facilitate education of emerging technologies through the creation of didactic materials and a curriculum for Zambian primary and high school students.

    2.

    Deliver immersive, hands-on, creative and empiric learning materials for learners to enhance knowledge, understanding and usefulness of STEM and 4th industrial revolution skills.

    3.

    Create reskilling and upskilling program for teachers to learn the Genius Curriculum
