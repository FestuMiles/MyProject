    About Us

    Genius Education Zambia is an EdTech, STEM organization whose mission is to promote, facilitate and enhance quality education. Genius Education Zambia works to bridge the gaps among high school students, young professionals, the workforce and the constantly evolving global environment. Our service at Genius Education Zambia is to promote STEM education in a holistic manner by working with stakeholders at all levels including governments, educators, researchers, corporate organizations, universities, schools and industry. We place a great importance on collaborative effort as well as intergenerational synergy.

    Our Vision

    To become a leading Edtech institution at the forefront of Science, Research and Innovation in Africa. We envision becoming the hub of Technological advancements in Africa. Our slogan is “ Smile At The future.”

    Our Mission

    To facilitate, enhance and promote quality Emerging STEM education.

    Our Core Values

    Creativity, Innovation, Education, Collaboration, Critical Thinking, Integrity, Community, and Passion.
